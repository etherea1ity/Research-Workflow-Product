Why this product exists

Not a chat wrapper. A research operating layer.

01

Workflow over vibes

Direct chat is fast, but complex product work needs stages, evidence, and explicit handoffs into the next action.

02

Context isolation

Brainstorming should not contaminate evidence. Temporary reasoning should not become long-term memory by accident.

03

Artifacts, not just answers

The output is designed for action: research briefs, competitor matrices, MVP specs, and issue bundles.

Technical edge

A product architecture users can actually feel.

The best commercial AI products make invisible system behavior visible. ScopeForge exposes its workflow, traces, evidence, and memory boundaries so users understand what the system is doing and why.
